Background

Ryan Burnett is a Brown University '22 graduate, currently employed at Paramount+ as a Senior Production Coordinator and QA Lead.

After obtaining a degree in Business at Brown, Ryan has centered his career around business, entreprenurship, and software development. In the Spring of 2025, Ryan will attend Georgia Tech part time to obtain his Masters in Computer Science through GA Tech's online program. When he is not hard at work programming or building keyboards , Ryan focuses his life on video editing, poetry, and writing. His artistic pursuits come to life with his YouTube channel - Mr. Lonely.

Ryan is passionate about QA Engineering and Front End Development and is working on a wide array of projects supported by the automated testing frameworks he is building.
